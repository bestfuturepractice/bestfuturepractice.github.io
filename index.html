About

Best Future Practice is a site dedicated to learning Computer Science where it will (hopefully) influence the industry in the next 5 years.

We dive into difficult topics such as distributed consensus in order to improve ourselves as programmers, and hopefully build less broken systems.

We provide (opinionated and focused) summaries of research papers. We annotate and cross-reference research. We run a monthly discussion group.

We don’t intend to cover everything. We’re focused on

  • distributed systems, weak and strong consensus; “medium data”
  • networking, data centers, Internet, and p2p
  • storage and file systems
  • operating systems and kernels
  • pragmatic cryptography, as it pertains to the above
  • security, where it’s related to building the above
  • visualization and analysis, where it helps us understand the above

(And that’s already a very wide field!)
